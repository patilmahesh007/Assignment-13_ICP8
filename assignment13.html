The story follows a group of strangers who find themselves stranded at a secluded cabin after their bus breaks down. As they wait for help to arrive, tensions rise and secrets begin to surface among the group. As the storm rages outside, paranoia and suspicion escalate inside the cabin. Strange occurrences and unexplained phenomena heighten the sense of unease, leading the characters to question each other's motives and sanity. Meanwhile, one of the passengers, a former detective haunted by a cold case from his past, becomes convinced that one of the others is a murderer hiding in plain sight among them. As he delves deeper into the mystery, he uncovers shocking revelations about each of the passengers, forcing them to confront their darkest secrets and deepest fears. As the storm worsens and the group's situation becomes increasingly desperate, they must band together to survive the night while also unraveling the chilling truth behind the cabin's sinister history and the identity of the killer in their midst.
